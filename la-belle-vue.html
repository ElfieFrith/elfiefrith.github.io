LA BELLE VUE

Unwind in a Prestigious Villa of Unmatched Grandeur

A Haven of Opulence

Welcome to La Belle Vue, where beauty unfolds at every turn. Perched high in the mountains, this architectural masterpiece offers breathtaking panoramic views of rolling hills and picturesque landscapes. Immerse yourself in the lap of luxury as you relax by the infinity pool or unwind in the private cinema room. La Belle Vue is an extraordinary villa that promises an extraordinary escape.

LIVING AREA

As you step inside the villa, you are greeted by a spacious and tastefully furnished living area. The open-plan layout creates a sense of fluidity and allows for effortless movement between the different spaces. The living room features plush sofas, sleek decor, and large windows that invite abundant natural light while offering picturesque vistas of the surrounding beauty.




KITCHEN

Adjacent to the living area is a fully equipped modern kitchen, complete with state-of-the-art appliances and ample counter space. Whether you are a culinary enthusiast or prefer the convenience of a private chef, this kitchen provides everything you need to prepare and enjoy delicious meals. A stylish dining area with a beautifully designed table awaits, creating an inviting ambiance for shared moments with loved ones.




MASTER SUITE

The villa boasts two exquisite bedrooms, each designed to provide a haven of tranquility. The master bedroom exudes opulence, featuring a king-size bed, premium linens, and an en-suite bathroom adorned with luxurious fixtures. From the master suite, step onto a private balcony or terrace and savor the breathtaking views that stretch out before you.

TWIN ROOM

The second bedroom, equally comfortable, offers twin beds and its own en-suite bathroom, ensuring privacy and comfort for all guests.

OUTSIDE AREA

Moving outside, you'll discover an enchanting outdoor area that epitomises luxury living. The villa is surrounded by meticulously landscaped gardens, lush greenery, and vibrant flowers, creating a serene oasis. The highlight is undoubtedly the private infinity pool, where you can take refreshing dips while gazing at the azure waters of the Aegean Sea. A sun-kissed terrace is furnished with comfortable loungers and plush outdoor seating, providing the perfect setting to bask in the Mediterranean sun or unwind with a book and a glass of champagne.
